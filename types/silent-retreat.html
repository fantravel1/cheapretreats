Common questions about silent retreats

Honest answers. No sales pitch.

What if I find silence unbearable?

This is more common than you might expect, and facilitators are prepared for it. You can speak with staff at any time. Many people find that the discomfort passes after the first day and is replaced by a surprising sense of relief.

Is a silent retreat the same as a meditation retreat?

Not necessarily. Some silent retreats include meditation, but others simply offer quiet rest without any practice or instruction. The shared element is the absence of social performance.

Can I bring my phone?

Policies vary. Some retreats collect phones on arrival; others ask you to keep them in your room on airplane mode. Check with your specific retreat for their policy.
